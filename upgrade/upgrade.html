Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can I try it out first?

Absolutely! You can try out all the paid features for free for one whole week, no credit card required. Activate free trial now.

Do you offer refunds?

We don't offer refunds. But, if you were mistakenly charged due to system error, contact us and we'll immediately issue you a refund.

Does it matter how many users I have?

Pricing for Plus and Premium plans is flat (it doesn't matter how many users you have). Pricing for Enterprise plan depends on the number of active users. All upgrades and payments are done on a per workspace basis.

What types of payment are supported?

We support only payments by credit card. Wire transfers and purchase orders aren't supported (except for the Server plan).

Can I switch between plans?

Yes. When you switch to a higher plan, you'll immediately get new features and be charged a prorated price for that month. When you downgrade, you'll be switched to the downgraded plan once your current (higher) plan ends.

Are there any additional taxes?

No, we take care of all the taxes. The price you see is what you'll get charged. You can enter your Tax ID, but it will only be used as an additional piece of information on your invoices.
